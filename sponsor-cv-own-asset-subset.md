# Sponsor CV Own-Asset Review

Date: 2026-05-04

## Scope

This note covers the subset of evergreen-only sponsors discussed in the portfolio-company removal review, with the CV question narrowed to sponsor-side use: a manager moving or recapitalizing assets it already owns into a continuation vehicle that it sponsors or continues to manage.

This excludes cases where the firm is only a buyer, LP, anchor investor, or co-investor in another manager's continuation vehicle.

## Bottom Line

No clear public evidence was found that any of the discussed evergreen-only sponsors have sponsored a continuation vehicle for assets they already owned.

Several firms have participated in third-party sponsor-led CVs, but that does not meet the narrowed definition.

## Discussed Sponsors With Public CV Participation, But Not Own-Asset Sponsor Evidence

- BCI: participated in Ethos Capital's Identity Digital continuation vehicle; Ethos was the sponsor.
- CBRE Investment Management: public materials show an active real estate secondaries / GP-led strategy, but no named CBRE-sponsored own-asset CV was identified.
- CPP Investments: active capital provider in multiple third-party continuation vehicles, including CVC Locron, Ardian/Syclef, Court Square/Playcore, PSG, Warburg Pincus, and I Squared/Inkia.
- GIC: appears as a capital provider / lead investor in third-party continuation vehicle transactions, but no clear own-asset sponsor-side CV was identified.
- J.P. Morgan Asset Management: led or anchored third-party sponsor CVs, including Apse Capital's continuation fund, but no clear JPMAM-sponsored own-asset CV was identified.
