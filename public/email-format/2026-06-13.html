Weekly Briefing – Infrastructure Sponsor M&A – 14 deals across Power & ET, Social Infra, Digital, Transportation, Utilities, and Midstream – June 6–12, 2026
GUGGENHEIM
INFRASTRUCTURE COVERAGE & ADVISORY
Infrastructure Sponsor M&A Activity
Weekly Briefing • June 6 – June 12, 2026
KEY THEMES
Power & ET led activity with 6 deals, while Social Infra followed with 3 deals. Power deployment centered on contracted renewables and storage, including Brookfield’s European renewables JV, its Vietnam partnership with Foxconn, CIP’s Devilla BESS stake sale, and Mubadala / Masdar’s Spanish renewables investment.
Direct North American deployment was concentrated in operating platforms and public-services infrastructure: Axium invested in Groupe Santé Sedna, La Caisse moved to full ownership of the A25 concession, Ridgewood acquired Dauntless Air, and KKR launched Helix Digital Infrastructure with more than $10bn of commitments.
Power & ET6 Deals
Mitsubishi HC Capital / Brookfield Renewables Platform | Brookfield Asset Management
Brookfield Asset Management (Joint Venture) · Renewable Power · Europe
Brookfield and Mitsubishi HC Capital launched a renewable-energy company seeded with 570 MW of contracted European wind and solar assets. Brookfield will operate the platform alongside Mitsubishi HC Capital, with the transaction carrying an initial equity value of approximately €400mm.
Source
 
Vietnam Renewable Energy Partnership | Brookfield Asset Management
Brookfield Asset Management (Joint Venture / Strategic Partnership) · Wind / Solar / Battery Storage · Vietnam
Brookfield and Foxconn formed a renewable-energy partnership in Vietnam targeting up to 1 GW of wind, solar, and battery storage. The platform is intended to support power supply for Foxconn and broader industrial customers as Vietnam adds contracted clean-power capacity.
Source
 
Repsol Spanish Renewables Portfolio | Mubadala
Mubadala / Masdar (Minority Stake) · Renewable Power · Spain
Masdar agreed to acquire a 49.99% stake in a 705 MW operating Spanish renewables portfolio from Repsol. The transaction creates a partnership around mature wind and solar assets and links Mubadala through Masdar ownership lineage.
Source
 
Devilla BESS Project | Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners
Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners (Minority Stake Sale) · Battery Storage · United Kingdom
CIP agreed to sell minority stakes in the 500 MW Devilla battery energy storage project to Scottish National Investment Bank and Nuclear Liabilities Fund. CIP remains the majority owner through Copenhagen Infrastructure IV while recycling capital into a large-scale UK storage project.
Source
 
Illinois Community Solar Portfolio | EQT Infrastructure
EQT Infrastructure / Cypress Creek Renewables (Portfolio Company Divestiture) · Community Solar · United States
EQT-backed Cypress Creek Renewables sold a 104 MW Illinois community solar portfolio to 38 Degrees North. The portfolio comprises 16 projects across 14 counties and reflects continued sponsor portfolio rotation in distributed solar assets.
Source
 
SK Group Renewable Energy JV | KKR
KKR (Joint Venture / Business Transfer) · Renewable Power · South Korea
SK and KKR signed a renewable-energy business transfer agreement covering solar and wind assets, with KKR expected to participate through asset acquisitions and capital contributions into an integrated renewable-energy entity. The deal adds sponsor capital to a Korean renewable platform with operating and development exposure.
Source
Social Infra3 Deals
Groupe Santé Sedna | Axium Infrastructure
Axium Infrastructure (Equity Investment / Strategic Partnership) · Healthcare Services · Canada
An Axium-managed fund made a new equity investment in Groupe Santé Sedna, a Canadian healthcare and social-services platform. Advisor materials identified a June 11 closing, and the transaction was treated as in-period despite a prior Competition Bureau reference.
Source
 
Dauntless Air | Ridgewood Infrastructure
Ridgewood Infrastructure (Acquisition) · Emergency Management / Wildfire Response · United States
Ridgewood Infrastructure acquired Dauntless Air, a provider of emergency-management infrastructure for wildfire response. The business supports aerial firefighting and mission-critical public-safety operations across North America.
Source
 
Navitas Credit Corp. | Wafra
Wafra (Acquisition / Definitive Agreement) · Infrastructure Services / Equipment Finance · United States
Wafra announced a definitive agreement to acquire Navitas Credit Corp., an equipment finance business. Inclusion rests on explicit Wafra Real Assets & Infrastructure involvement rather than the target operating in a traditional infrastructure vertical.
Source
Digital2 Deals
Helix Digital Infrastructure | KKR
KKR (Platform Launch / Anchor Equity Capitalization) · AI Infrastructure / Data Centers · North America
KKR launched Helix Digital Infrastructure with partners as a new company to finance and deliver AI infrastructure. The platform announced more than $10bn of commitments targeting data centers, power, transmission and distribution, fiber, and connectivity assets.
Source
 
Ansan Data Center Site / SEL5 | Stonepeak
Stonepeak / Digital Edge (Portfolio Company Acquisition) · Data Center Site · South Korea
Stonepeak-backed Digital Edge secured a fully powered land parcel in Ansan, South Korea for its planned SEL5 data center. The site supports a 60 MW development in a power-constrained market and expands Digital Edge’s Seoul-area data-center pipeline.
Source
Transportation1 Deal
A25 Concession | La Caisse
La Caisse (Acquisition) · Toll Road / Bridge Concession · Canada
La Caisse agreed to acquire Transurban’s remaining 50% interest in the A25 Concession, increasing its ownership from 50% to 100%. The Montreal-area asset includes toll road and bridge infrastructure under a long-term concession framework.
Source
Utilities1 Deal
University of Kentucky Central Utility Plant | La Caisse
La Caisse / Plenary Americas (P3 Financial Close) · Campus Utility Infrastructure · United States
Kentucky Infrastructure Partners reached financial close on the University of Kentucky Central Utility Plant P3 project. The deal includes sponsor equity alongside bond financing and is linked to La Caisse through Plenary Americas infrastructure-platform lineage.
Source
Midstream1 Deal
Anwim / MOYA Fuel Station Network | Stonepeak
Stonepeak / Energy Equation Partners (Acquisition) · Fuel Distribution / Logistics · Poland
Stonepeak and Energy Equation Partners agreed to acquire Anwim, operator of the MOYA fuel station network in Poland. The transaction gives Stonepeak direct exposure to fuel distribution and logistics infrastructure with a national retail and wholesale platform.
