Weekly Briefing – Infrastructure Sponsor M&A – 19 deals across Transportation, Power & ET, Digital, Utilities, Midstream, and Social Infra – May 9–May 15, 2026
GUGGENHEIM
INFRASTRUCTURE COVERAGE & ADVISORY
Infrastructure Sponsor M&A Activity
Weekly Briefing • May 9 – May 15, 2026
KEY THEMES
Digital and power platforms drove the week’s largest capital commitments, including Blackstone Tac Opps’ $1.0bn VoltaGrid investment, Apollo’s Hornsea 3 consortium, the BXDC IPO, and Goldman Sachs Alternatives Infrastructure’s QScale acquisition.
Transportation activity remained broad across asset finance, rail equipment, aviation services, bus transit, and air freight, with sponsor-led transactions from Stonepeak, Basalt, Igneo, TPG Rise Climate TI, Brookfield Private Equity, and H.I.G. Infrastructure.
Power & ET5 Deals
Siruai Wind and Storage Project | Meridiam
Meridiam (Platform Launch) · Wind + Battery Storage · Kenya
Meridiam and Craftskills Energy launched the Siruai Wind and Storage project in Kenya, combining 100 MW of wind generation with a 50 MWh battery energy storage system. The project is located near the Kipeto wind farm in Kajiado County and is backed by more than EUR200mm of investment.
Source
 
VoltaGrid | Blackstone Tac Opps
Blackstone Tac Opps (Minority Stake) · Distributed Power / Data Center Power · United States
VoltaGrid signed agreements for a $1.0bn strategic equity investment from funds managed by Blackstone Tactical Opportunities and Halliburton. The investment includes $775mm of primary capital and a $225mm secondary purchase, supporting behind-the-meter power generation for AI data centers, microgrids and industrial applications.
Source
 
Hornsea 3 Offshore Wind Farm | Apollo
Apollo (Minority Stake) · Offshore Wind · United Kingdom
Mubadala announced a US$325mm investment in Ørsted's Hornsea 3 offshore wind farm alongside a consortium led by Apollo-managed funds. Hornsea 3 is expected to deliver 2.9 GW of capacity off the Norfolk coast in the United Kingdom, with Ørsted retaining 50% ownership and leading development, construction and operation.
Source
 
187 MW Taiwan Solar Portfolio | GIP / BlackRock
GIP / BlackRock (Sale - Buyout) · Solar · Taiwan
J&V Energy agreed to acquire a 187 MW operational solar portfolio in Taiwan from a fund managed by Global Infrastructure Partners, part of BlackRock. The operating portfolio expands J&V Energy's renewable power platform in Taiwan.
Source
 
HydePoint / Nymo | Vergia / Swiss Life AM
Vergia / Swiss Life AM (Minority Stake) · Offshore Energy Infrastructure · Norway
Nymo became a co-owner and industrial partner in HydePoint through a collaboration agreement. The transaction adds Nymo to HydePoint's industrial ecosystem alongside Moreld, Kongsberg Maritime, Vergia / Swiss Life Asset Managers and CIFO Management.
Source
Digital4 Deals
Kao Data Park Royal Site | Infratil
Infratil (Bolt-On) · Data Centers · United Kingdom
Kao Data acquired a 4.7-acre brownfield site on the former Frogmore Industrial Estate in Park Royal, West London, from ReAssure Limited for a planned data center development. The facility is expected to support AI and advanced computing workloads and go live in 2029, subject to planning.
Source
 
QScale | Goldman Sachs Alternatives Infrastructure
Goldman Sachs Alternatives Infrastructure (Buyout) · Data Centers · Canada
Infrastructure at Goldman Sachs Alternatives closed the acquisition of QScale, a Canadian developer and operator of next-generation, AI-ready data centers. QScale's founders and management are reinvesting alongside Goldman Sachs Alternatives and will continue to lead the business.
Source
 
Blackstone Digital Infrastructure Trust Inc. (BXDC) | BXDC
BXDC (IPO) · Data Centers · United States
Blackstone Digital Infrastructure Trust Inc. priced its initial public offering at $20.00 per share, with shares expected to begin trading on the NYSE on May 14, 2026 under ticker BXDC. The company is focused on acquiring and owning stabilized, income-generating data center assets leased to hyperscale tenants.
Source
 
255 Caspian Drive | Brookfield Properties
Brookfield Properties (Bolt-On) · Data Centers · United States
Brookfield Properties acquired the data center at 255 Caspian Drive in Sunnyvale, California from DivcoWest for $90.3mm, according to county recorder filings reported by The Real Deal. Equinix is the tenant in the 120,000-square-foot building.
Source
Transportation6 Deals
BMO Transportation and Vendor Finance | Stonepeak
Stonepeak (Buyout) · Transportation Asset Finance · United States / Canada
Stonepeak reached an agreement to acquire BMO's Transportation Finance and Vendor Finance businesses, including related loan portfolios in the United States and Canada. BMO will reinvest for a 19.9% minority interest, while the platform continues to provide financing for trucks, trailers and other transportation and vendor equipment.
Source
 
R.E.L.A.M. | Basalt
Basalt (Buyout) · Rail Equipment Leasing · United States / Canada
Funds advised by Basalt Infrastructure Partners agreed to acquire R.E.L.A.M., a North American lessor of maintenance-of-way and hi-rail equipment, from Paceline Equity Partners. The company operates a scaled fleet of specialized rail maintenance assets serving railroads, contractors and other rail infrastructure operators.
Source
 
Corporate Air | Igneo
Igneo (Bolt-On) · Aviation FBO · United States
Infinity Aviation Group acquired Corporate Air at Vero Beach Regional Airport in Florida, expanding its fixed-base-operator network into South Florida. The facility includes eight hangars, a large ramp area, a passenger terminal and on-site customs clearance.
Source
 
Dysons | TPG Rise Climate TI / Foresight
TPG Rise Climate TI / Foresight (Bolt-On) · Bus Transit · Australia
Kinetic entered into an agreement to acquire 100% of Dysons, an Australian bus and coach operator serving Victoria and New South Wales. The transaction expands Kinetic's bus network and zero-emissions transport strategy and is expected to complete in the second half of 2026 subject to approvals.
Source
 
World Freight Company | Brookfield Private Equity
Brookfield Private Equity (Buyout) · Air Freight Services · Global
Brookfield, through its private equity business, agreed to acquire World Freight Company from PAI Partners and EQT. WFC is a global air freight services provider and general sales and service agent serving airlines, freight forwarders and trade lanes across more than 80 countries.
Source
 
International Aerospace Coatings | H.I.G. Infrastructure / Tiger Infrastructure Fund III
H.I.G. Infrastructure / Tiger Infrastructure Fund III (Buyout / Sale - Buyout) · Aviation Services · United States / Ireland
H.I.G. Capital completed the acquisition of International Aerospace Coatings from Tiger Infrastructure Partners. IAC provides aircraft painting and aviation technical services from a global footprint of 25 hangars across 11 sites in the United States and Europe.
Source
Utilities2 Deals
Terrafame Sotkamo Electrical Grid Assets | Equitix
Equitix (Bolt-On) · Industrial Power Networks · Finland
Aurora Infrastructure entered into a strategic partnership with Terrafame by acquiring the high- and medium-voltage electrical grid assets at Terrafame's Sotkamo industrial site in Finland. The transaction is structured as a buy-and-lease-back arrangement supporting industrial-site power infrastructure.
Source
 
Verene Energia S.A. | La Caisse Infrastructure
La Caisse Infrastructure (Joint Venture) · Power Transmission · Brazil
La Caisse and Grupo Energía Bogotá entered into a final agreement to create a jointly controlled 50/50 power transmission platform in Brazil under the Verene Energia S.A. name. The combined platform will include 26 electric transmission concessions and more than 9,000 km of transmission lines across 17 Brazilian states.
Source
Midstream1 Deal
Caturus / Commonwealth LNG | Ares Infrastructure Opportunities
Ares Infrastructure Opportunities (Platform Launch) · LNG Export · United States
Caturus made a positive final investment decision for the Commonwealth LNG project in Louisiana, including the successful closing of $9.75bn in project financing for a 9.5 mtpa LNG export facility. Mubadala Energy is an equity participant, CPP Investments is increasing its stake, and major financial partners include an Ares Infrastructure Opportunities fund.
Source
Social Infra1 Deal
Noble Environmental | Apollo
Apollo (Majority Stake) · Waste / Renewable Natural Gas · United States
Apollo-managed funds acquired a majority interest in Noble Environmental, a vertically integrated waste-management platform headquartered in Pittsburgh. Noble provides waste collection, hauling, transfer and disposal services and operates renewable natural gas facilities at landfill gas locations.
