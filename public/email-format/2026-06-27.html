Weekly Briefing – Infrastructure Sponsor M&A – 12 deals across Power & ET, Transportation, Utilities, and Digital – June 20–26, 2026
GUGGENHEIM
INFRASTRUCTURE COVERAGE & ADVISORY
Infrastructure Sponsor M&A Activity
Weekly Briefing • June 20 – June 26, 2026
KEY THEMES
Power & ET remained the center of activity, accounting for 7 of 12 deals across U.S. renewables and storage, German offshore wind, UK BESS and solar, and Japanese wind development. Transportation and utilities each contributed two transactions; digital narrowed to Antin-backed Eurofiber’s Berlin fiber divestiture.
U.S. deployment centered on named operating platforms and project capital: KKR moved on EDF Power Solutions’ U.S. and Canadian operations, Pantheon / Igneo backed Terra-Gen, and TPG-backed Matrix Renewables secured DESRI preferred equity for solar and storage projects.
Power & ET 7 Deals
EDF Power Solutions U.S. and Canada operations | KKR
KKR (Acquisition) · Renewable Power / Storage / EV Charging · United States / Canada
EDF signed an agreement for KKR to acquire EDF Power Solutions assets and operations in the U.S. and Canada, including renewable power, storage, EV charging, and microgrid activities. The transaction gives KKR a scaled North American clean power operating platform.
Source
 
Tormes Solar and Alamo BESS projects | TPG
TPG (Preferred Equity / Project-Level Minority Equity Investment) · Solar / Battery Storage · United States
TPG-backed Matrix Renewables secured a $210mm preferred equity commitment from DESRI for the Tormes Solar and Alamo BESS construction portfolio. The financing supports U.S. solar and battery storage development through an existing renewable infrastructure platform.
Source
 
Terra-Gen | Igneo Infrastructure Partners / Pantheon
Igneo / Pantheon (Co-Investment) · Renewables Platform · United States
Pantheon Infrastructure committed approximately $55mm to Terra-Gen through an Igneo-managed co-investment vehicle. Terra-Gen owns and develops utility-scale wind, solar, and battery storage assets across the U.S. power market.
Source
 
Gennaker offshore wind project | BlackRock / GIP
BlackRock / GIP (Minority Stake Sale) · Offshore Wind · Germany
Skyborn Renewables agreed to sell Stadtwerke München a 25% equity interest in the Gennaker offshore wind project. Skyborn is a GIP infrastructure portfolio company within BlackRock’s infrastructure platform, and Gennaker is positioned as a major German Baltic Sea offshore wind development.
Source
 
Yarnton BESS | Schroders Greencoat
Schroders Greencoat (Asset Acquisition) · Battery Storage · United Kingdom
A Schroders Greencoat / Wessex Gardens renewable infrastructure fund acquired the 50 MW Yarnton battery energy storage system in Oxfordshire from Rivington Energy. The transaction adds a grid-scale storage asset in a constrained UK power market.
Source
 
Gresham House UK solar portfolio | True Green Capital Management
True Green Capital Management (Operating Asset Acquisition) · Solar · United Kingdom
A UK entity managed by True Green Capital acquired a 20.3 MWdc operating solar portfolio from Gresham House Renewable Energy VCT 1 and VCT 2. The portfolio adds contracted operating solar assets to True Green’s European distributed generation footprint.
Source
 
TerraWind / Sym Energy Miyazaki wind JV | Stonepeak
Stonepeak (Joint Venture via Portfolio Company) · Onshore Wind · Japan
Stonepeak-backed TerraWind Renewables formed a jointly owned special purpose company with Sym Energy for wind development in Miyazaki, Japan. TerraWind is an onshore wind platform in which Stonepeak holds an 80% interest.
Source
Transportation 2 Deals
Specialist Fleet Services | Astatine Investment Partners
Astatine Investment Partners (Bolt-On) · Fleet Services · United Kingdom
Astatine-backed NRG Riverside / NRG Fleet Services agreed to acquire the entire issued share capital of Specialist Fleet Services from Paragon Banking Group. SFS adds specialist contract hire, fleet management, and municipal fleet capabilities to NRG Riverside’s UK platform.
Source
 
Integmar Marine Technologies | Antin Infrastructure Partners
Antin Infrastructure Partners (Portfolio Company Acquisition) · Marine Safety Technology · Türkiye
Antin-backed Consilium Safety Group acquired Integmar Marine Technologies, expanding its presence in Türkiye and its marine safety technology offering. Consilium was acquired by an Antin Flagship Fund V affiliate, bringing the bolt-on into an infrastructure-backed safety platform.
Source
Utilities 2 Deals
Southern Water | Asterion Industrial Partners / Macquarie Asset Management
Asterion / Macquarie (Minority Stake) · Regulated Water Utility · United Kingdom
Asterion joined a Macquarie-led consortium committing £1.2bn of equity support to Southern Water, including Asterion’s approximately £300mm commitment for an approximately 20% minority shareholding. The transaction supports a regulated UK water and wastewater utility’s investment programme.
Source
 
Amprion indirect stake | Swiss Life Asset Managers
Swiss Life Asset Managers (Sale) · Electricity Transmission · Germany
Swiss Life Asset Managers signed an agreement to sell an indirect stake in Amprion, one of Germany’s major electricity transmission system operators. The sale recycles capital from a regulated power transmission asset held through the manager’s infrastructure platform.
Source
Digital 1 Deal
Eurofiber Netz Berlin | Antin Infrastructure Partners
Antin Infrastructure Partners (Portfolio Company Divestiture) · Fiber-to-the-Home · Germany
Antin-backed Eurofiber sold Eurofiber Netz GmbH in Berlin to MIH Group, effective June 22, 2026. The business develops and operates fiber-to-the-home infrastructure in the Berlin metropolitan area with more than 150,000 homes passed.
Source
