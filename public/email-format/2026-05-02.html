Infrastructure Sponsor M&A Activity
Weekly Briefing • May 2 – May 8, 2026
KEY THEMES
Energy transition activity remained broad-based this week, spanning project acquisitions, portfolio-company M&A, and minority growth capital. Nuveen-backed Verdian added 117 MWp of ready-to-build agrivoltaic projects in Sicily, BayWa r.e. sold its Italian Power Solutions platform to Fervo, IFM-backed Mobius acquired Air Liquide biogas assets across four countries, Mubadala invested in Power Factors, and GIP-backed Skyborn moved to consolidate control of Nordergründe.
Capital formation and sponsor rotation were also active. EQT formed a $1.3bn cold-storage JV with Americold, Mubadala committed $300mm alongside Stonepeak behind Textainer/Seaco, CBRE IM-backed Accelerate raised $630mm, KKR and XPV agreed to sell Axius Water, CPP Investments disclosed a 5.09% stake in NEXTDC, and Ember-backed H2O Innovation added bestUV.
Power & ET 5 Deals
117 MWp Sicily Agrivoltaic Portfolio | Verdian / Nuveen Infrastructure
Nuveen Infrastructure (Portfolio Company Acquisition) · Solar / Agrivoltaics · Italy
Verdian, a clean-energy IPP controlled by Nuveen Infrastructure, acquired a 117 MWp portfolio of three ready-to-build agrivoltaic projects in Sicily from Genertec International Holding. The projects are located in Ramacca and Paternò, have secured FER X contracts under a CfD structure with GSE, and are expected to begin construction toward the end of 2026 with operations targeted between mid- and late-2027.

BayWa r.e. Power Solutions | Fervo / Energy Infrastructure Partners
EIP (Portfolio Company Divestiture) · Solar EPC / Energy Services · Italy
Fervo acquired BayWa r.e. Power Solutions from BayWa r.e. AG, an EIP-linked renewable-energy platform. The Verona-based business develops and builds rooftop and ground-mounted PV systems, storage systems, and integrated energy solutions for industrial customers, utilities, and IPPs, with more than 120 MWp installed and annual revenue exceeding €20mm.
Source
 
Power Factors | Mubadala
Mubadala (Minority Investment) · Renewable Energy Software · Global
Mubadala announced a significant minority investment in Power Factors alongside existing investor Vista Equity Partners. Power Factors provides a renewable energy management suite for wind, solar, and storage operators, supporting more than 600 customers across 18,000 sites in over 70 countries and managing approximately 310 GW of capacity.
Source
 
Air Liquide Biogas Assets | Mobius Renewables / IFM
IFM (Portfolio Company Acquisition) · RNG / Biogas · US & Europe
Air Liquide agreed to divest its biogas production activities in the United States, France, Norway, and Sweden to Mobius Renewables, the low-carbon fuels platform established by IFM-managed funds. The carve-out includes production units and distribution assets and expands Mobius beyond its initial GreenGasUSA platform.
Source
 
Nordergründe Offshore Wind Farm | Skyborn / GIP
GIP (Portfolio Company Acquisition) · Offshore Wind · Germany
Public merger-control disclosure showed Skyborn Renewables, a Global Infrastructure Partners portfolio company, seeking the indirect acquisition of a 70% stake and sole control of OWP Nordergründe GmbH & Co. KG. Nordergründe is an operating offshore wind project in Germany with 111 MW of capacity.
Source
Digital 2 Deals
NEXTDC | CPP Investments
CPP Investments (Minority Stake) · Data Centers · Australia
CPP Investments disclosed a substantial holding in NEXTDC Limited, Australia’s listed data-center operator, representing 36,750,320 ordinary shares and 5.09% voting power. The position follows NEXTDC’s recent equity issuance and capital plan to support accelerated data-center development.
Source
 
Accelerate Infrastructure Opportunities | Mubadala / CBRE IM
CBRE IM / Mubadala (Primary Capital Raise) · Infrastructure Sites · US
CBRE Investment Management-backed Accelerate Infrastructure Opportunities closed a $630mm primary capital raise, bringing total equity commitments to $1.26bn. The round included additional investments from funds managed by CBRE IM and new capital from Mubadala, Australian Retirement Trust, and other global investors to support a site platform across digital, renewable, and transportation infrastructure.
Source
Transportation 2 Deals
Americold North American Cold Storage JV | EQT
EQT (Joint Venture) · Cold Storage / Logistics · North America
Americold and EQT formed a $1.3bn North American cold-storage joint venture backed by EQT’s Active Core Infrastructure fund. Americold will contribute 12 facilities totaling approximately 124mm cubic feet of temperature-controlled capacity and more than 400,000 pallet positions, with EQT acquiring a 70% interest and Americold retaining 30% and day-to-day management.
Source
 
Textainer / Seaco | Mubadala / Stonepeak
Mubadala / Stonepeak (Co-Investment) · Container Leasing · Global
Mubadala announced a $300mm co-investment alongside Stonepeak to support Textainer’s acquisition of Seaco. The investment backs the combination of two container leasing businesses and creates one of the world’s largest container leasing platforms, with the combined fleet serving shipping lines across global trade routes.
Source
Utilities 2 Deals
Axius Water | KKR / XPV
KKR / XPV (Sale) · Water Infrastructure · North America
KKR and XPV Water Partners signed a definitive agreement to sell Axius Water to CRH. KKR and XPV formed Axius in 2019 to unify specialized nutrient-management and water-quality businesses, and the transaction provides CRH with a scaled platform in advanced water-quality solutions.
Source
 
bestUV | H2O Innovation / Ember Infrastructure
Ember Infrastructure (Portfolio Company Bolt-On) · Water Treatment · Netherlands
H2O Innovation, an Ember Infrastructure portfolio company, acquired bestUV, a Dutch manufacturer of ultraviolet water-disinfection systems. The bolt-on expands H2O Innovation into UV disinfection and adds proprietary reactors serving municipal, industrial, maritime, and other water-treatment applications.
