Infrastructure Sponsor M&A Activity
Weekly Briefing • April 11 – April 17, 2026
KEY THEMES
Seven deals closed but the week was notably light on large-cap activity — I Squared’s $650mm acquisition of Spire’s gas storage assets stood out as the largest disclosed transaction.
Direct investments from I Squared (Spire Storage) and TPG (Züm) were matched by sponsor exits from Macquarie (Evryo), CIP (Summerfield BESS), and Patrizia (SEI).
Power & ET 3 Deals
Summerfield Battery Energy Storage Project (240 MW / 960 MWh) | CIP
CIP (Divestiture) · Battery Storage · Australia
CIP divested its 100% equity stake in the Summerfield Battery Energy Storage Project to Palisade Investment Partners. The asset is a utility-scale 240 MW / 960 MWh lithium-ion battery system located in South Australia.
Source
 
Sustainable Energy Infrastructure (SEI) | Patrizia
Patrizia (Divestiture) · Distributed Solar & Storage · Australia
Patrizia signed a binding agreement to fully divest its 100% equity stake in Sustainable Energy Infrastructure (SEI) to CleanPeak Energy. SEI owns, develops and operates a distributed portfolio of sub-5 MW solar arrays and battery energy storage systems.
Source
 
Insplorion Hydrogen Sensor Business | Antin
Antin / Consilium Safety Group (Bolt-On) · Hydrogen Sensors · Europe
Consilium Safety Group, a portfolio company of Antin Infrastructure Partners, acquired Insplorion’s hydrogen sensor technology business, including related intellectual property and physical assets for advanced hydrogen detection sensors.
Source
Transportation 2 Deals
Züm | TPG
TPG (Growth Equity) · Student Mobility · US
TPG made a $100mm strategic growth equity investment into Züm, an operator of electric school bus fleets and mass student mobility networks, valuing the specialized transportation infrastructure company at $1.7bn.
Source
 
Berland Resource Road & Home Creek Road | CBRE
CBRE / Canadian Resource Roadways (Bolt-On) · Resource Roads · Canada
Canadian Resource Roadways, a portfolio company of CBRE Investment Management, signed a definitive agreement to acquire a 90% equity stake in a 134-kilometer industrial access road network that supports regional forestry and energy operations.
Source
Midstream 1 Deal
Spire Storage | I Squared
I Squared (Asset Acquisition) · Natural Gas Storage · US
I Squared Capital agreed to acquire the midstream natural gas storage assets of Spire Inc. for $650mm in cash and deferred payments. The assets provide up to 72 Bcf of combined working gas capacity.
Source
Utilities 1 Deal
Evryo Group (Distribuție Energie Oltenia) | Macquarie
Macquarie (Divestiture) · Electricity Distribution · Europe
Macquarie Asset Management agreed to divest its 100% equity interest in Evryo Group to Premier Energy Group. Evryo operates a regulated electricity distribution network in Romania spanning 80,000 kilometers and connecting 1.5mm customers.
