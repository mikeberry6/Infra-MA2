Weekly Briefing – Infrastructure Sponsor M&A – 26 deals across Power & ET, Digital, Transportation, Utilities, and Midstream – June 27–July 3, 2026
GUGGENHEIM
INFRASTRUCTURE COVERAGE & ADVISORY
Infrastructure Sponsor M&A Activity
Weekly Briefing • June 27 – July 3, 2026
KEY THEMES
Power and digital infrastructure drove the week, accounting for 18 of 26 deals. Sponsor capital targeted contracted power and storage platforms, including Partners Group-backed PowerTransitions, MSIP at Greenlight, Macquarie / Eku Energy, GIP / Skyborn at Nordergrunde, Actis / CVC DIF at Klara, and CPP / EQT behind EdgeConneX.
U.S. deployment remained concentrated in operating assets, platform add-ons, and public-market exits: Blackstone sold interests in three Northern Virginia data centers, Brookfield / Generate rotated Ubiquity's Southern California fiber network, EnCap Flatrock exited Cardinal Midstream assets, Oaktree-backed ITG completed its Nasdaq IPO, and Partners Group-backed PowerTransitions signed Roseton.
Power & ET 9 Deals
Roseton Generating Facility | Partners Group
Partners Group / PowerTransitions (Add-On Acquisition) · Power Generation · United States
Partners Group-backed PowerTransitions agreed to acquire the 1,242 MW Roseton Generating Facility in New York. The add-on expands the platform's operating power-generation footprint in a capacity-constrained U.S. market.
Source
 
Greenlight Electricity Centre | Morgan Stanley Infrastructure Partners
MSIP / Pembina / Kineticor (Ownership Interest / Growth Equity) · Power Generation · Canada
Pembina, MSIP, and Kineticor reached FID on the 932 MW Greenlight Electricity Centre in Alberta. MSIP acquired OPTrust's 50% interest, taking a 47.5% project stake, with remaining costs funded partly through owner equity contributions.
Source
 
Dion BESS | Macquarie Asset Management
Macquarie Asset Management / Eku Energy (Project Acquisition / Development Agreement) · Battery Storage · Germany
Eku Energy announced its German market entry through acquisition and development rights for the 400 MW / 1,600 MWh Dion battery storage project in Lamspringe. Eku is owned by BCI and a Macquarie Asset Management-managed fund.
Source
 
SK / KKR Korea renewables platform | KKR
KKR / SK (Platform Joint Venture) · Renewable Power · South Korea
KKR and SK signed definitive agreements to establish a renewable-energy platform in South Korea. The investment is tied to KKR's Asia Pacific infrastructure strategy and targets power-transition assets in a high-demand market.
Source
 
Kallista Energy | APG / Ardian
APG / PKZH / Ardian (Minority Stake Acquisition / Exit) · Renewables Platform · France
APG, on behalf of its infrastructure pool and alongside PKZH, acquired Ardian's remaining 38% interest in Kallista Energy. The transaction moves a French onshore wind, solar, and battery-storage platform from Ardian ownership to APG-led control.
Source
 
Klara Renewables | Actis / CVC DIF
Actis / CVC DIF (Platform Acquisition / Divestiture) · Onshore Wind / Solar / BESS · Poland
Actis agreed to acquire Klara Renewables from CVC DIF, launching a Polish renewables platform anchored by 171 MW of operating onshore wind assets. The portfolio also carries solar PV and battery-storage hybridization potential.
Source
 
Nordergrunde offshore wind farm | Global Infrastructure Partners
GIP / Skyborn Renewables (Add-On Acquisition) · Offshore Wind · Germany
GIP-backed Skyborn Renewables agreed to acquire the remaining 70% equity interest in the Nordergrunde offshore wind farm. The transaction gives Skyborn full ownership of the German operating asset and deepens its offshore wind platform.
Source
 
Enersol solar portfolio | La Caisse / Equitix
Velto Renewables / Equitix (Minority Stake Acquisition / Sale) · Solar PV · Spain
La Caisse-backed Velto Renewables agreed to acquire Equitix's minority stake in the Enersol solar portfolio in Andalusia. The deal gives Velto full ownership of nine operating PV assets totaling 45 MWp.
Source
 
Cobirgy biogas plant | CIP
CIP (Final Investment Decision) · Biomethane / Biogas · Spain
Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners took FID on Cobirgy through Advanced Bioenergy Fund I. The project adds large-scale Spanish biomethane and biogas infrastructure to CIP's energy-transition portfolio.
Source
Digital 9 Deals
EQT AI Infrastructure / EdgeConneX | CPP Investments
CPP Investments / EQT (Strategic Equity Investment) · Data Centers · Global
CPP Investments closed a US$1.75bn investment alongside EQT to support global digital infrastructure growth led by EdgeConneX. The transaction provides scaled equity backing for AI-driven data-center expansion.
Source
 
Northern Virginia data centers | Blackstone
Blackstone / Digital Realty (Partial Equity Interest Sale) · Data Centers · United States
Digital Realty agreed to purchase Blackstone-affiliated funds' blended 64% equity interest in three fully leased Northern Virginia data centers. The facilities represent 288 MW of IT capacity in the leading U.S. data-center market.
Source
 
ITG Inc. | Oaktree Capital
ITG / Oaktree Capital (IPO) · Digital Infrastructure · United States
Oaktree-backed ITG priced its U.S. IPO and began public trading on Nasdaq during the target week. The listing crystallizes public-market equity access for a digital infrastructure platform backed by Oaktree's power and infrastructure strategy.
Source
 
Lefdal Mine Datacenter | 3i Infrastructure
3i Infrastructure (Incremental Stake Acquisition) · Data Centers · Norway
3i Infrastructure agreed to acquire a further 23% stake in Lefdal Mine Datacenter alongside 3i-managed co-investors. LMD is an operating Norwegian data-center campus built around low-cost renewable power and cavern-based infrastructure.
Source
 
Nippon Gateway Infrastructure | DigitalBridge
DigitalBridge / JEXI (Platform Formation) · Data Centers · Japan
DigitalBridge and JEXI formed Nippon Gateway Infrastructure, a new Japanese colocation data-center platform. NGI launched with data-center assets acquired from NEC and is positioned for Japan's digital infrastructure demand.
Source
 
Kao Data | Infratil
Siemens Financial Services / Kao Data (Minority Stake Sale) · Data Centers · United Kingdom
Siemens Financial Services made a minority equity investment in Kao Data, the UK data-center platform backed by Infratil and Legal & General. The capital supports expansion of Kao's high-performance computing and AI-ready campus footprint.
Source
 
I-2SEA submarine cable system | I Squared Capital
Lightstorm / Microsoft (Joint Venture) · Subsea Cable · India / Southeast Asia
I Squared-backed Lightstorm signed a joint build agreement with Microsoft for the I-2SEA submarine cable system. The project adds new India-to-Southeast Asia subsea capacity for cloud, AI, and enterprise connectivity demand.
Source
 
Volta Data Centres | Ardian
Ardian / Verne / Arcus (Portfolio Company Sale) · Data Centers · United Kingdom
Ardian-backed Verne agreed to sell 100% of Volta Data Centres to Arcus European Infrastructure Fund 4. Volta operates a central London data center and gives Arcus an established urban colocation asset.
Source
 
Ubiquity Southern California fiber network | Brookfield / Generate Capital
Intrepid Fiber / Ubiquity (Portfolio Company Acquisition / Divestiture) · Fiber · United States
Brookfield Infrastructure-backed Intrepid Fiber Networks agreed to acquire Ubiquity's Southern California fiber network. The deal transfers FTTP network assets from Generate-backed Ubiquity to an active U.S. fiber expansion platform.
Source
Transportation 5 Deals
UK passenger rolling stock platform | Partners Group
Partners Group (Infrastructure Secondaries Investment) · Rail Rolling Stock · United Kingdom
Partners Group made a co-lead infrastructure secondaries investment in a UK passenger rolling stock leasing platform. The platform comprises more than 1,500 vehicles contracted into the UK rail system.
Source
 
Ocean Yield | KKR
KKR / A.P. Moller Holding (Sale) · Maritime Leasing · Norway / Global
A.P. Moller Holding agreed to acquire 100% of Ocean Yield from KKR-managed funds. Ocean Yield owns maritime infrastructure assets under long-term charters, making the sale a full exit from KKR's shipping-infrastructure platform.

Avenue Capital aviation leasing portfolio | Partners Group
Partners Group (Infrastructure Secondaries Investment) · Aviation Leasing · Global
Partners Group invested US$250mm as sole lead investor in a continuation vehicle acquiring a global commercial aviation leasing portfolio. The contracted aircraft-leasing exposure fits the firm's infrastructure secondaries strategy.
Source
 
GeniePoint EV charging locations | EQT Infrastructure
EQT Infrastructure / InstaVolt (Bolt-On Acquisition) · EV Charging · United Kingdom
EQT-backed InstaVolt agreed to acquire 228 GeniePoint EV charging locations from Equans. The transaction adds more than 260 chargers and moves InstaVolt past 1,000 UK sites.
Source
 
InstaVolt Iberian EV charging business | Antin / EQT Infrastructure
Powerdot / InstaVolt (Business Operations Sale) · EV Charging · Spain / Portugal
Antin-backed Powerdot agreed to acquire InstaVolt's Spanish and Portuguese operations from EQT-backed InstaVolt. The divestiture transfers the Iberian charging network and related employees to a regional EV infrastructure platform.

GIC sold an 11.03% equity stake in Genus Power Infrastructures through an open-market block transaction. Genus supplies smart-metering and utility digitalization infrastructure, making the sale a secondary equity rotation in the power distribution value chain.

Drammen Fjernvarme | Vauban Infrastructure Partners

Vauban-backed Oslofjord Varme agreed to acquire Å Energi's remaining 50% stake in Drammen Fjernvarme. The transaction moves the Norwegian district-heating business to full Oslofjord Varme ownership.

EnCap Flatrock-backed Cardinal Midstream agreed to sell Cardinal Delaware Basin and Cardinal New Mexico to San Mateo Midstream for US$752mm. The assets include natural gas gathering and processing infrastructure in the Delaware Basin.
