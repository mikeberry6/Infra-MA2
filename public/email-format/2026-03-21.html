GUGGENHEIM
INFRASTRUCTURE COVERAGE & ADVISORY
Infrastructure Sponsor M&A Activity
Weekly Briefing • March 21 – March 27, 2026
KEY THEMES
The week was led by Brookfield / La Caisse’s ~$9.0bn take-private of Boralex, the clear size outlier in a week that produced 11 qualifying deals across six sectors.
Utilities and grid-adjacent assets accounted for three deals, spanning transmission (AllianzGI), smart metering (Igneo), and district cooling (Stonepeak).
Utilities 3 Deals
Amprion | Allianz Global Investors
AllianzGI (Minority Stake) · Electricity Grid · Germany
AllianzGI agreed to acquire an indirect stake in Amprion from Talanx; Amprion’s extra-high-voltage grid spans 11,000+ km and serves ~29mm people. Amprion plans to invest more than €36bn by 2029 to construct or upgrade 9,300 km of power lines to support the energy transition.
Source
 
OnStream | Igneo Infrastructure Partners
Igneo Infrastructure Partners (Acquisition) · Smart Metering · UK
Igneo entered a binding agreement to acquire OnStream, the UK’s second-largest smart meter asset provider, with ~7mm installed meters and a ~3mm-meter pipeline. OnStream also operates ~60,000 meters in Germany.
Source
 
Lestari Cooling Energy Sdn. Bhd. | Stonepeak
Stonepeak / KJTS / KWAP (Growth Equity / Minority Stake) · District Cooling · Malaysia
KJTS and Stonepeak added KWAP as an investor in Lestari Cooling Energy, with KWAP expected to commit up to ~RM190mm. The platform develops energy-efficient district cooling systems across Malaysia, targeting up to 50% reductions in energy consumption and over 70,000 tCO2 in annual emissions savings at full deployment.
Source
Power & ET 3 Deals
Boralex Inc. | Brookfield / La Caisse
Brookfield / La Caisse (Take-Private) · Renewables · Canada
Brookfield and La Caisse agreed to acquire Boralex for $37.25/share in cash, implying ~$3.8bn of equity value and ~$9.0bn EV, with La Caisse owning 30% post-close. Boralex operates ~3,783 MW of installed wind, solar, hydro, and storage capacity across Canada, the US, France, and the UK, with an 8.2 GW development pipeline.
Source
 
Akira Portfolio (6 Italian Solar PV Plants) | J.P. Morgan Asset Management
Sonnedix / J.P. Morgan Asset Management (Bolt-On) · Solar · Italy
Sonnedix acquired the 194 MW Akira portfolio from EOS Investment Management and Capital Dynamics, comprising six solar PV projects in Lazio, including five operating plants and one 12 MW project under construction. All six assets benefit from long-term PPAs, and the transaction lifted Sonnedix’s operating capacity in Italy above 1 GW.
Source
 
Tamil Nadu Solar Project (100 MW) | ADIA Infrastructure
ReNew Energy Global (Divestiture) · Solar · India
ReNew agreed to sell its 100 MW / 117.5 MWp Tamil Nadu solar project to Technique Solaire at ~$49mm EV; the asset has a 25-year TANGEDCO PPA. The project was commissioned in 2019 and operates under a PPA priced at ₹3.47/kWh.
Source
Transportation 2 Deals
Safeway Concessions (9 Indian Toll Highways) | Macquarie Asset Management
Macquarie Asset Management (Divestiture) · Toll Roads · India
VINCI Highways agreed to acquire Safeway Concessions from Macquarie Asia Infrastructure Fund 2 at ~INR150bn EV; the portfolio comprises nine toll concessions spanning nearly 700 km. The concessions are operated under TOT contracts with NHAI in Andhra Pradesh and Gujarat, with maturities ranging from 2048 to 2058.
Source
 
A63 Motorway | InfraRed Capital Partners / CVC DIF
InfraRed Capital Partners / CVC DIF (Divestiture) · Toll Road · France
Abertis acquired the remaining 49% of the A63 motorway from HICL Infrastructure, CVC DIF and NGE Autoroutes, taking ownership to 100%. The A63 is a 104 km toll motorway connecting Salles to Saint-Geours-de-Marenne, a strategic corridor between Bordeaux and the Basque Country.
Source
Social 2 Deals
All American Grease Services | Goldman Sachs Asset Management
LES / Goldman Sachs Asset Management (Bolt-On) · Waste Services · US
LES acquired All American Grease Services Companies, a non-hazardous liquid waste company offering grease trap and used cooking oil collection services across the Gulf Coast and Southeast US. The acquisition added five service branches and three wastewater treatment facilities to LES’s network of 300+ vacuum trucks and 24 treatment plants.
Source
 
Equinox Growers Greenhouse Facility | Generate Capital
Generate Capital (Divestiture) · Controlled-Environment Agriculture · US
Generate sold Equinox Growers to Taylor Farms, monetizing a 10-acre Virginia greenhouse capable of producing approximately 6mm pounds of leafy greens annually for East Coast markets. Developed in 2022 and operating at scale, the facility supports year-round regional food supply and highlights controlled-environment agriculture as a critical form of agricultural infrastructure.
Source
Digital 1 Deal
Desktop S.A. | H.I.G. Capital
H.I.G. Capital (Divestiture) · Fiber Broadband / ISP · Brazil
H.I.G. signed a definitive agreement to sell Desktop to Claro at R$4.0bn (~$750mm, including debt); Desktop serves 1.2mm+ subscribers through 58,000+ km of fiber. Desktop is a leading fiber-based ISP in São Paulo state, where it operates as the dominant broadband provider across the interior.
