Weekly Briefing – Infrastructure Sponsor M&A – 14 deals across Power & ET, Social Infra, Digital, Transportation, and Midstream – May 30–June 5, 2026
GUGGENHEIM
INFRASTRUCTURE COVERAGE & ADVISORY
Infrastructure Sponsor M&A Activity
Weekly Briefing • May 30 – June 5, 2026
KEY THEMES
Power & ET was the dominant activity pocket with 9 of 14 deals, spanning renewable platforms, battery storage, distributed generation, EV charging, public-market equity placements, and sponsor exits in power assets.
Direct capital deployment remained visible in North America: GIP led equity for Delfin FLNG 1, Igneo invested in Pathway Power, QIC backed InCharge Energy, and CVC DIF-backed GS Power Partners secured tax equity for a 41 MWdc community solar portfolio.
Power & ET 9 Deals
INNIO Group | ADIA
ADIA (IPO / Secondary Share Sale) · Distributed Power Generation · Austria / United States listing
INNIO Group completed its Nasdaq debut through an upsized IPO in which AI Alpine, co-owned by Advent-managed funds and ADIA, sold 90mm shares. INNIO supplies distributed power-generation engines and related services for data centers, microgrids, grid stabilization, and industrial energy applications.
Source
 
Vena Energy India | Global Infrastructure Partners
Global Infrastructure Partners / Vena Group (Portfolio Company Divestiture) · Renewable Power / Battery Storage · India
Vena Group entered into a definitive agreement to sell 100% of Vena Energy India Holdings to Inox Clean Energy. The India platform includes 5.4 GW of solar and wind assets and a 2.5 GWh BESS renewable-energy pipeline tied to GIP III's original infrastructure acquisition of Equis Energy.
Source
 
Pathway Power | Igneo Infrastructure Partners
Igneo Infrastructure Partners (Preferred Equity Investment) · Renewable Power / Battery Storage · United States
Igneo completed a preferred equity investment in Pathway Power, a U.S. utility-scale renewable and storage development platform. Pathway has assembled more than 1 GW of projects expected to begin construction in 2026-2027, plus approximately 1,000 MW of early-stage hybrid interconnection positions across SPP and MISO.
Source
 
Enderby Battery Storage Project | EIG
EIG / Fidra Energy (Portfolio Company Acquisition) · Battery Storage · United Kingdom
EIG-backed Fidra Energy acquired the Enderby battery storage project from Innova. The Leicestershire project is expected to deliver up to 1,025 MW once operational and increases Fidra's UK BESS pipeline to more than 4 GW.
Source
 
Mountain Peak / Canyon Peak Power | Blackstone
Blackstone (Sale) · Gas-Fired Power · United States
TransAlta signed a purchase and sale agreement to acquire Mountain Peak Power and Canyon Peak Power from indirect subsidiaries of Blackstone. The transaction covers two fully contracted gas-fired peaking facilities in Colorado, adding dispatchable power capacity under existing contracts.
Source
 
ACME Solar Holdings | ADIA
ADIA (Primary Capital Raise / QIP) · Utility-Scale Solar · India
ACME Solar completed an INR 2,800 crore qualified institutional placement with ADIA named among participating investors. ACME develops, owns, operates, and maintains utility-scale renewable-energy projects across India.
Source
 
GS Power Community Solar Portfolio | CVC DIF
CVC DIF / GS Power Partners (Tax Equity Financing) · Community Solar · United States
CVC DIF-backed GS Power Partners secured $51mm of tax equity financing for a 41 MWdc community solar portfolio across New York, Maryland, and Illinois. The portfolio-company financing close adds equity capital to distributed solar infrastructure within DIF Infrastructure VII lineage.
Source
 
InCharge Energy | QIC
QIC (Strategic Investment / Growth Equity) · EV Charging / Distributed Energy · North America
InCharge Energy announced a $46mm strategic investment led by S2G with participation from QIC. The company provides EV charging, fleet electrification, electrical infrastructure, and distributed energy resource solutions across North America.
Source
 
Dajin Heavy Industry | UBS Asset Management
UBS Asset Management (IPO / Cornerstone Investment) · Offshore Wind Equipment · China / Hong Kong
Dajin Heavy Industry began trading in Hong Kong during the target period, with UBS Asset Management Singapore identified among cornerstone investors. The company manufactures offshore wind foundations, towers, and related equipment for energy-transition infrastructure.
Source
Social Infra 2 Deals
Broadway Proper | Harrison Street
Harrison Street (Sale) · Senior Living · United States
Harrison Street sold Broadway Proper, a 232-unit independent and assisted living community in Tucson, Arizona. Berkadia announced the completed sale during the target period, marking a realized social-infrastructure exit rather than a financing transaction.
Source
 
Teleconsult and Meditecs | EQT Infrastructure
EQT Infrastructure / Evidia (Portfolio Company Acquisition) · Healthcare Diagnostics · Europe
EQT Infrastructure-backed Evidia acquired Teleconsult and Meditecs as bolt-ons to its diagnostic imaging and radiology services platform. Evidia traces to EQT Infrastructure Fund V, and the acquisitions expand healthcare-services infrastructure in Europe.
Source
Digital 1 Deal
LineoX | Asterion Industrial Partners
Asterion Industrial Partners / Axion (Sale) · Rural Microwave Backhaul · Spain
Telefonica reached an agreement to acquire LineoX, a rural microwave backhauling platform, from Asterion-backed Axion / LineoX. The transaction traces to Asterion Industrial Infra Fund I and adds digital connectivity infrastructure to Telefonica's rural network footprint.
Source
Transportation 1 Deal
CLI | Macquarie Asset Management
Macquarie Asset Management / MIP V (Sale) · Port / Logistics Infrastructure · Brazil
Macquarie Asset Management and IG4 signed a definitive agreement to sell a controlling stake in CLI - Corredor Logistica e Infraestrutura S.A. to AD Ports Group. The transaction values the Brazilian infrastructure and logistics operator at approximately US$835mm.
Source
Midstream 1 Deal
Delfin FLNG 1 | Global Infrastructure Partners
Global Infrastructure Partners (Growth Investment / Project JV Equity Commitment) · LNG Export / FLNG · United States
Delfin Midstream announced a $5bn final investment decision for Delfin FLNG 1, the first offshore LNG export facility in the United States. GIP, part of BlackRock, led the investor group committing equity alongside Vitol and other partners for the first phase of the project.
Source
