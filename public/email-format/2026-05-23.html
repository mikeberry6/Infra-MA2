Infrastructure Sponsor M&A Activity
Weekly Briefing • May 23 – May 29, 2026
KEY THEMES
Transportation led with 4 of 10 deals, concentrated in road concessions, InvIT interests, and EV charging. Key deployment markers were Patria’s R$1.19bn Eixo SP co-investment, Macquarie Asset Management’s Maple Infrastructure Trust acquisition, and EQT / GIP’s Voltera-Revel combination.
Digital was the clearest North American direct-deployment theme: I Squared Capital’s $225mm Cogent Fiber seed portfolio and NOVA / DartPoints’ Lexington campus both add data center capacity, with DartPoints citing a 20-30 MW initial path and 70 MW long-term potential.
Transportation 4 Deals
Eixo SP Co-Investment | Patria
Patria (Co-Investment) · Road Concession · Brazil
Patria concluded an R$1.19bn asset-specific co-investment for Eixo SP, with Silk Road Fund as the sole investor and capital routed through a co-investment vehicle linked to Patria Infraestrutura IV. Eixo SP operates a 1,273 km Sao Paulo road concession spanning 12 highways and 62 municipalities, with a 30-year concession and R$14bn investment plan.
Source
 
Maple Infrastructure Trust Units | Macquarie Asset Management / La Caisse
Macquarie Asset Management / La Caisse (Secondary Acquisition) · Road InvIT · India
Macquarie Asset Management’s MAIF 4 vehicle agreed to acquire up to 177,289,950 Maple Infrastructure Trust units, alongside related Maple IM and Maple PM equity interests, from La Caisse’s CDPQ Infrastructures Asia III. Maple owns and operates Indian road assets through government concessions, giving the transaction a direct roads-infrastructure exposure.
Source
 
Voltera / Revel Combined EV Charging Platform | EQT Infrastructure / GIP
EQT Infrastructure / GIP (Business Combination) · EV Charging / E-Mobility · United States
EQT-backed Voltera and GIP-backed Revel entered into a definitive agreement to combine their businesses into an urban fast-charging infrastructure platform. The combined company is expected to include more than 1,000 charging stalls operational and under development across 11 major U.S. metro markets, with EQT as majority owner and GIP retaining a minority stake.
Source
 
Five Indian Road SPVs | Actis
Actis (Portfolio Company Acquisition) · Roads / Toll Bridge · India
Nxt-Infra Trust’s investment manager approved execution of securities purchase agreements covering shareholdings and beneficial ownership in Darah Jhalawar Highways, Vadodara Kim Expressway, Calicut Expressway, Vindhyachal Expressway, and Second Vivekananda Bridge Tollway. The assets add a five-asset roads and bridge package to the Actis-sponsored long-life infrastructure vehicle.
Source
Power & ET 2 Deals
76 MWp Uruguay Solar Portfolio | Ardian
Ardian (Buyout) · Solar · Uruguay
Ardian Clean Energy Evergreen Fund acquired two operating solar PV plants in Uruguay with a combined capacity of 76 MWp. The assets mark ACEEF’s entry into Uruguay and expand Ardian’s Latin American renewable-power footprint, with AGR-AM managing the portfolio.
Source
 
Stoneworthy BESS Project | Equitix
Equitix (Portfolio Company Acquisition) · Battery Storage · United Kingdom
Eelpower Energy, the Equitix-led battery-storage platform backed with Aware Super and the UK National Wealth Fund, acquired the 50 MW / 100 MWh Stoneworthy BESS project in Devon from RES. Construction is slated to begin in 2027, with commercial operations planned for 2028.
Source
Digital 2 Deals
Cogent Fiber Data Center Portfolio | I Squared Capital
I Squared Capital (Buyout) · Data Centers · United States
I Squared Capital entered into a definitive purchase and sale agreement to acquire ten data center facilities from Cogent Fiber for $225mm in cash. The 53 MW, 259,000-square-foot seed portfolio spans nine U.S. markets and anchors a new AI inference and edge colocation platform, with I Squared committing up to $1bn for expansion and follow-on acquisitions.
Source
 
Lexington Data Center Campus | NOVA Infrastructure
NOVA Infrastructure (Portfolio Company Acquisition) · Data Centers · United States
NOVA-backed DartPoints acquired a Lexington, Kentucky data center campus spanning approximately 343,000 square feet on 29.5 acres, including about 81,000 square feet of existing raised-floor data center space and an owned on-site substation. The campus has a 20-30 MW initial path and longer-term expansion potential to 70 MW for AI, neo-cloud, hyperscale, and enterprise workloads.
Source
Utilities 1 Deal
Naturgy Energy Group | CVC
CVC (Sale) · Integrated Gas / Power Utility · Spain
CVC sold its entire 13.8% stake in Naturgy Energy Group through an accelerated bookbuild and related derivative settlement. The exit covered a Spanish integrated gas and power utility stake worth around €4bn at prevailing market prices and followed GIP / BlackRock’s earlier full exit from Naturgy.
Source
Social Infra 1 Deal
Estia Health | Stonepeak
Stonepeak (Majority Stake) · Residential Aged Care · Australia
A Stonepeak-led consortium agreed to acquire a majority stake in Estia Health from Bain Capital, extending Stonepeak’s Volarae Living social-infrastructure platform in Australia and New Zealand. Estia is one of Australia’s largest residential aged-care operators, with more than 90 homes across four states and approximately 9,000 residents.
