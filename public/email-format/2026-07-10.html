GUGGENHEIM
INFRASTRUCTURE COVERAGE & ADVISORY
Infrastructure Sponsor M&A Activity
Weekly Briefing • July 4 – July 10, 2026
KEY THEMES
Power & ET led with 8 of 18 deals, spanning platform-scale deployment and capital rotation. EQT Infrastructure signed for Copia Power, EIP-backed Alpiq agreed to acquire 90% of Harmony Energy, and Apollo agreed to exit its Great Bay Renewables interests for approximately US$390 million. MidOcean Energy drew the week's largest disclosed commitment at US$1.13 billion.
U.S. deployment combined operating platforms, portfolio-company acquisitions and project equity across Copia Power, Sabey Data Center Properties, Dresser Utility Solutions, Milestone Environmental, Blue Whale Materials, Watertown Solar and the NuGen portfolio. Apollo's Great Bay transaction was an exit, while Harmony Energy, D-Marin, TERRAS, Nicollin, Paprec Metabader, Northern Fiber and the Kundl campus provided the principal European contrast.
Power & ET 8 Deals
Great Bay Renewables | Apollo
Apollo / Northampton Capital Partners (Sale) · Renewable Energy Royalties · United States
Apollo Infrastructure Funds signed an agreement to sell their 50% membership interests in Great Bay Renewables to Northampton Capital Partners for approximately US$390 million. The divestiture transfers Apollo's stake in the renewable-energy royalty platform.
Source
 
Copia Power | EQT Infrastructure / Carlyle Infrastructure
EQT Infrastructure / Carlyle Infrastructure (Buyout / Sale) · Integrated Power & AI Infrastructure · United States
EQT Infrastructure VII agreed to acquire Copia Power from Carlyle Infrastructure. The platform has 2.6 GW of generation and storage operating or under construction, with more than 9 GW of data-center development supported by over 25 GW of solar and storage and 7 GW of gas generation.
Source
 
Harmony Energy | Energy Infrastructure Partners
Energy Infrastructure Partners / Alpiq (Portfolio Company Acquisition) · Battery Storage · Europe
EIP-backed Alpiq agreed to acquire 90% of Harmony Energy, with co-founder and CEO Peter Kavanagh retaining 10%. The platform adds approximately 400 MW under construction and a pipeline exceeding 12 GW across the United Kingdom, France, Germany and Poland.
Source
 
Watertown Solar | EnCap Investments
EnCap Investments / Linea Energy / Crux (Tax Equity Investment) · Utility-Scale Solar · Michigan, United States
EnCap Energy Transition Fund II-backed Linea Energy closed tax-equity financing with Crux for the 172 MWdc Watertown Solar project in Michigan, comprising a single-investor commitment from Crux. The equity closing is separate from the project's previously announced debt package.
Source
 
Blue Whale Materials | Ara Partners
Ara Partners / Blue Whale Materials (Growth Equity) · Battery Recycling & Critical Minerals · United States
Ara Partners made an upsized equity commitment to Blue Whale Materials alongside a separate senior secured credit facility from Breakwall Capital. The Oklahoma battery-recycling platform is scaling annual processing capacity to 20,000 metric tons of feedstock.
Source
 
NuGen C&I Solar Portfolio | Argo Infrastructure Partners
Argo Infrastructure Partners / NuGen (Portfolio Acquisition) · Commercial & Industrial Solar · United States
Argo acquired eight operating commercial and industrial solar sites from NuGen, including six in Massachusetts and two in New Jersey. The add-on expands Argo's distributed-solar platform to 196 sites and approximately 270 MW of operating capacity.
Source
 
Castets Solar Project | Energy Infrastructure Partners
Energy Infrastructure Partners / BayWa r.e. (Portfolio Company Divestiture) · Solar PV · France
EIP-backed BayWa r.e. transferred the 16.8 MWp Castets solar project to a local French consortium while remaining responsible for engineering, procurement, construction and long-term operations and maintenance.
Source
 
Lemvig Biogas | APG
APG / Palisade / BioticNRG (Portfolio Company Acquisition) · Biogas / Biomethane · Europe / Denmark
APG-backed BioticNRG acquired Lemvig Biogas, an industrial-scale Danish facility operating since 1992. BioticNRG plans to upgrade the plant to produce biomethane for injection into Denmark's gas grid.
Source
Social Infra 3 Deals
Nicollin Environnement | Morgan Stanley Infrastructure Partners
MSIP (Majority Stake) · Waste & Water Environmental Services · France
MSIP committed to acquire a majority stake in Nicollin Environnement, with the Nicollin family remaining invested. The French platform serves approximately 360 public contracts and 8,000 private clients with 4,800 employees, 2,900 vehicles and a national network of environmental-infrastructure sites.
Source
 
Paprec Metabader | Vauban Infrastructure Partners / Swiss Life Asset Managers / UBS Asset Management
Vauban Infrastructure Partners / Swiss Life Asset Managers / UBS Asset Management / Paprec (Joint Venture / Portfolio Company Combination) · Recycling & Environmental Services · Europe / Switzerland
Vauban-backed Paprec Swiss Group combined with Metabader to create Paprec Metabader, which has 31 plants and agencies across Switzerland. Jessy Bader became CEO and a shareholder; the combined company's capital also includes a Swiss Life- and UBS-managed infrastructure fund.
Source
 
Milestone Environmental | I Squared Capital
I Squared Capital / SK Capital (Buyout) · Environmental Services · United States
I Squared Capital's flagship fund agreed to acquire Milestone Environmental from SK Capital. The platform owns and operates 15 fully permitted disposal facilities across major U.S. energy basins and manages approximately one billion gallons of waste annually across the energy and industrial sectors.
Source
Digital 2 Deals
Sabey Data Center Properties | Ares Management
Ares Management (Minority Stake) · Data Centers · United States
Ares Secondaries funds made a minority equity investment in Sabey Data Center Properties. The U.S. platform spans six energized campuses with approximately 251 MW of operating capacity and more than four million square feet of mission-critical space.
Source
 
Northern Fiber Holding | iCON Infrastructure
iCON Infrastructure / Strategic Fiber Networks (Portfolio Company Acquisition) · Fiber Networks · Germany
iCON-backed Strategic Fiber Networks agreed to acquire Northern Fiber Holding in a share deal. Operating through the Lünecom and sewikom brands, Northern Fiber manages owned and leased networks serving rural and semi-urban communities across three German states.
Source
Transportation 2 Deals
D-Marin | InfraVia Capital Partners
InfraVia Capital Partners / CVC (Buyout / Sale) · Marina Infrastructure · Europe / Mediterranean / EMEA
InfraVia European Fund VI agreed to acquire D-Marin from CVC alongside management. The premium-marina platform operates and develops 28 marinas with more than 14,300 berths across nine countries.
Source
 
TERRAS | H.I.G. Capital
H.I.G. Capital (Majority Stake) · Infrastructure Engineering & Construction · Europe / Germany
H.I.G. Capital agreed to acquire a majority stake in TERRAS, with the founders reinvesting alongside the buyer. The DACH platform delivers civil infrastructure, specialty foundation engineering, rail construction and related services across mobility, energy, digital, water and urban-development markets.
Source
Utilities 2 Deals
Kundl Life Science Campus | Swiss Life Asset Managers
Swiss Life Asset Managers / Gelsenwasser / INNEXIS (Portfolio Company Acquisition) · Industrial Site Infrastructure & Services · Europe / Austria
Swiss Life- and Gelsenwasser-backed INNEXIS agreed to acquire the Kundl Life Science Campus from Novartis, including selected infrastructure facilities, assets and real estate-related services. The site spans approximately 264,000 square metres and more than 100 buildings; Novartis will retain the research and production facilities.
Source
 
Dresser Utility Solutions | Blackstone
Blackstone Energy Transition Partners (Buyout) · Utility Network Equipment · United States
Blackstone Energy Transition Partners entered a definitive agreement to acquire Dresser Utility Solutions from First Reserve. The approximately 850-employee platform supplies metering, control, instrumentation and repair products used across gas and water utility networks.
Source
Midstream 1 Deal
MidOcean Energy | EIG Global Energy Partners
EIG Global Energy Partners / MidOcean Energy (Primary Capital Raise) · LNG Infrastructure · North America / Global
The Private Department of Sheikh Mohammed bin Khalid Al Nahyan committed US$1.13 billion to EIG-managed MidOcean Energy, expanding the LNG platform's institutional shareholder base. MidOcean reports a balance sheet above US$5 billion and interests in LNG projects across the Americas and Australia.
