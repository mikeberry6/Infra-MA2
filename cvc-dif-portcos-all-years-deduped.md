## Duplicate / Alias Decisions

- Bernhard, LLC -> ENFRA. Bernhard legally rebranded as ENFRA in 2025 with no ownership or legal-structure change.
- Green Street Power Partners -> GS Power Partners. GS Power Partners is the current brand for the former Green Street Power Partners platform.
- Tonaquint Data Centers -> ValorC3. Tonaquint rebranded as ValorC3 Data Centers in 2025.
- Etobicoke Healthcare Partnership -> Etobicoke General Hospital (Phase 1 Patient Tower). Etobicoke Healthcare Partnership is the project consortium/developer label for the same hospital PPP asset.
- Robson Communities Utilities / Pima Utility -> JW Water Holdings (incl. Robson Utilities). CVC DIF acquired JW Water and the Robson utilities concurrently to create one Arizona regulated water/wastewater platform.

## Not Treated As Duplicates

- Airtower Networks, Joink, RFNOW Inc., and Valley Fiber Ltd. are separate digital-infrastructure platforms.
- CARMA Corp. and Diverso Energy Inc. are separate Canadian building/energy infrastructure platforms.
- BluEarth Renewables, Brahms Wind, Canadian Hills Wind, Idaho Wind Partners, and Kingfisher Wind are distinct renewable-power assets/platforms.
